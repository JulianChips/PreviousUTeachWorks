Switch or Stay:

If we look at the outcomes you can have for staying, you have a 1/3 chance of getting the prize. But if you switch, the probability doubles to 2/3 chance!
That is because if you choose the prize and stay, you win.(1(Prize)/3) However if you choose wrong and switch, you win.(2(Goats)/3)
